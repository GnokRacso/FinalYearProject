University


Due to the Ministry of Education’s efforts to expand the education industry, Malaysia’s education sector has experienced substantial growth. The Malaysian government has made it a long-term goal for Malaysia to become a regional center for excellence in education.

Ministry


Malaysia’s politics is conducted within the framework of a federal representative democratic monarchy. The prime minister of Malaysia is the head of government. Executive power is exercised by the federal government and 13 state governments. Federal legislative power belongs to the Federal Parliament and 13 state assemblies.
